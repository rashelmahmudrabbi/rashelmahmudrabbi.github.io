MediVision – AI Skin Disease Classifier

End-to-end deep learning web application for skin lesion classification with LIME-based visual explanations. Trained on the HAM10000 dataset, this system provides clinically interpretable AI diagnostics for dermatological conditions, making AI decisions transparent and trustworthy for medical practitioners.

CNN + Transfer Learning pipeline for land-use classification on EuroSAT satellite imagery. Supports 10 land-use categories with 96.4% accuracy. Demonstrates effectiveness of fine-tuned pre-trained models on remote sensing tasks.
